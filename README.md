<h1>Call of Duty: Mobile</h1>

<p>Call of Duty: Mobile is a popular first-person shooter game released in 2019. It is available on Android and iOS and features multiplayer and battle royale modes.</p>

<h2>Game Overview</h2>
<p>The game combines classic Call of Duty gameplay with mobile controls. Players can compete in fast-paced matches using different weapons, maps, and characters.</p>

<h2>Game Modes</h2>
<ul>
  <li>Team Deathmatch – eliminate enemies to score points</li>
  <li>Frontline – similar to Team Deathmatch but fixed spawn points</li>
  <li>Domination – capture and hold control points</li>
  <li>Search and Destroy – plant or defuse bombs</li>
  <li>Battle Royale – survive against many players</li>
</ul>

<h2>How to Play</h2>
<p>Players control their character using on-screen buttons. The goal depends on the mode, such as eliminating enemies or completing objectives.</p>

<h2>Scoring System</h2>
